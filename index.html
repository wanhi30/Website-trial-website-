Visual Narratives & Printmaking Practices

Exploring Indigenous Pnar Identity, Oral History, and the Contemporary World

Between Two Worlds

To me, Art is a reflection of one's thoughts, experiences, and knowledge. I belong to the indigenous Pnar tribe from Meghalaya. We do not have written histories but rather an oral history through folktales, songs, and myths.

My work explores what these stories mean to the people, how they shape our philosophy, and how we navigate the transition between traditional values and modern influences.
